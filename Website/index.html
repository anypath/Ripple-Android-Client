Why use the Instant Ripple Wallet?

  • Secure
  • Fast
  • Reliable
  • Easy to Use

With Instant Ripple wallet you log directly into the Ripple Protocol network from you smartphone in a dedicated app. This way you are not storing your RippleTrade login or password on our servers somewhere. Your account is as secure as your device and Ripple’s network.

Experience the speed and security that the Ripple network brings in transferring value. Whethere it’s XRP, Bitcoin, Fiat, or other cryptos, you can now experience it where ever you go. Download the app on Google Play Store.

Features to Include:

  • Simple registration or activation of existing Ripple accounts
  • Display current values of: xrp, IOUs, and Fiats
  • Ability to convert from/to: xrp, IOUs, and Fiats via Gateway of choice
  • Ability to send and receive via QR-codes, NFC, and addresses
  • Address book
  • Notification for received xrp, IOUs, and/or Fiats
  • Ability to turn off/on quality of rippling and set the rippling quality
  • Ability to select gateways (enable or disable) and update trustlines
  • Ability to setup user profile: picture, name, contact, address, phone, ripple address
  • When payment is received, the user can automatically convert received xrp, IOUs, and Fiats into preferred IOUs from gateway of choice
