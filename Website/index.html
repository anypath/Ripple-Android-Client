Why use the Instant Ripple Wallet?

  • Secure
  • Fast
  • Reliable
  • Easy to Use

Experience the speed and security that the Ripple protocol brings in transferring value. Whether it is XRP, Fiat, BTC or other cryptocurrencies, you can now experience the power of the network wherever you go. Download the app on the Google Play Store today.

With our Instant Ripple wallet, you log directly into the Ripple network from your smartphone in our dedicated app. You are not storing your Ripple Trade login or password on our servers. Your account is as secure as your device and Ripple’s network.

Features to Include:

  • Simple registration or activation of existing Ripple accounts
  • Display current balances
  • Ability to convert between currencies via gateway of choice
  • Ability to send and receive funds via QR-codes, NFC, and addresses
  • Contacts book
  • Notifications for received funds
  • Ability to toggle rippling and set the rippling quality
  • Ability to select gateways and update trust settings
